Intro To The Project

The Maverse is driven by the community and our NFT holders are considered as shareholders of the project which will receive a dividend from our future game income in each quarter.

We are the first project using the Free-To-Earn (F2E) business model and we will launch a mahjong game on both IOS and Android platforms. This game will be available to play for everyone even if you do not own our NFTs.

Tokenomics

Each ERC-20 token launched by The Maverse will be backed up by Bitcoin, we aim to reduce the risk for both NFT and token holders. The Maverse promises to purchase Bitcoin with funds from NFT sales and future game income.

All players, no matter if they have NFTs, can exchange their game coins with The Maverse bank for ERC-20 tokens. Tokens will be available to trade on secondary markets such as Binance, Uniswap in the future.
